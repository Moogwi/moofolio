The 'Sorcières' project revolves around the portrayal of women in art. Developed as part of my motion design bachelor's degree validation, it comprises both a video and an editorial piece inspired by the historical magazine, 'Sorcières.' The accompanying text for the video is drawn directly from the magazine's editorial. Through this project, my aim was to convey a powerful message while celebrating the portraits of influential women in art history.
