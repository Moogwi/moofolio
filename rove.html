Rove

360

Rove is a project undertaken over a period of 6 months by a team of three individuals. Our goal was to explore the fusion of cultures and the experiences of multicultural individuals. We envisioned an event venue that would curate content and activities centered around this theme, encompassing music, dance, exhibitions, and participatory workshops. Our aim was to spotlight multicultural artists who infuse their diverse backgrounds into their work.

Split into three phases, our project initially focused on conceptualization, followed by design, and ultimately, production. Our objective was to develop a visual identity that celebrates cultural diversity without succumbing to stereotypes or discrimination. We chose to center our work around languages and alphabets to create a graphic language that could be applied across all our deliverables.
