Core is an innovative exploration in the field of home automation, blending nature-inspired artistic direction with cutting-edge technology. Our concept relies on the use of bio-inspired sensors to transform traditional objects into intelligent elements, offering a new user experience. This task was completed within a three-week timeframe, showcasing our commitment to ecological transition and our ability to create intuitive, eco-friendly and futuristic solutions.

We delivered various components, including a landing page-style launch website, media content for social networks, an advertising campaign, an application for device use, and animated content, including some in 3D. Each deliverable was crafted with consideration for the creative axes and the DNA we established, aiming to achieve a diverse yet cohesive ensemble.
