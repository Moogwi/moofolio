neoverse

design system
interface

Neoverse is a project aimed at developing a design system tailored for Forumactif formats, a platform that's both widely utilized and frequently underrated. Drawing inspiration from the aesthetic of glass materials, I incorporated elements of transparency and luminosity to infuse the design with a contemporary feel. Through this project, I got to widen my skills in system design, ensuring both visual appeal and functional coherence while addressing the platform's unique requirements.

What I find compelling about Forumactif platforms is that they integrate all the activities I enjoy into a single interface. From graphic design to shaping a universe, the objective is to animate everything within the platform and engage users in an immersive experience that fuels their imagination. Forum interfaces are structured differently from conventional web pages, offering an opportunity to rethink interface design by breaking away from certain norms.
